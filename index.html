Sound key

  • lh = "lly" (million)  ·  nh = "ny" (canyon)
  • ã / -ão = nasal "ow" through the nose  ·  ç = soft "s"
  • Final unstressed -e is barely voiced
  • s before a consonant or at word-end = "sh"
  • São = "sowng" (saint)  ·  Lagoa = lake
